Research

Analysis of the Volatility of China Financial Markets Based on the View of Hurst Statistics
Advised by Prof. Weilin Xiao, I participated in the National Statistical Modelling competition in China. We built a new statistics using MATLAB to estimate the Hurst Parameter of China's finance market.
Our paper won the provincial first prize.
Solar Panel Energy Prediction Study Using Machine Learning

I participated in the GEARS program at NC State University, advised by Dr. Majed.
As a team of two, we analyzed and predicted the solar panel energy consumption in the U.S., and proposed possible references of installation decision.
Our work won an Honorable Mention.
